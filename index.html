About HullODL

More and more people are using their mobile devices to browse the web, and we need to make sure our websites are ready.

An open device lab gives you the freedom of testing on a range of devices, without the cost.

If you want to come down and use the lab, give us a tweet a few days beforehand @HullODL.

Want to help out? Great! Check out the devices we need or find another way you can help out.
